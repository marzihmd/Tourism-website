About London:

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is one of the world's most dynamic and historically rich cities. Known for its iconic landmarks, diverse culture, and vibrant arts scene, London offers a wealth of experiences for both residents and tourists.




Ancient London:

London's history dates back to Roman times when it was known as Londinium, founded around AD 43. The Romans built the city's first significant infrastructure, including roads, walls, and public buildings.
