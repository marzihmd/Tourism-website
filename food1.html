Buckingham Palace Tour and Afternoon Tea


Combine two London must-do activities with a Buckingham Palace Tour and Afternoon Tea this summer. Feel like royalty as you wander the grand and iconic 19 State Rooms in Buckingham Palace, the official residence of King Charles III. End the day with afternoon tea, the ultimate British tradition, delighting your senses with delicious savoury treats and sweet freshly baked goods.

Chinatown

Chinatown in London is a vibrant and culturally rich area located in the heart of the West End, near Leicester Square and Soho. It is a bustling hub known for its Chinese restaurants, shops, and cultural festivities.

Restaurants:
Chinatown boasts a wide array of Chinese and East Asian restaurants offering diverse cuisines from different regions of China and beyond, including dim sum, Szechuan, Cantonese, and more.

Shops:
There are numerous Asian supermarkets, bakeries, and specialty stores selling Chinese goods, including traditional medicine, herbs, teas, and gifts.

Cultural Sites:
The area features several Chinese cultural landmarks, including pagodas, Chinese gates (paifangs), and decorations, especially during Chinese New Year.
